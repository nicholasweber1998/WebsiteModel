1991 MX - 5

This is where the car really shines is its drivability. It may not have a lot of power, but it makes up for being light weight. One of the reasons why it corners well is the 50 50 weight distribution front to back. The rear wheel drive and independent suspension make for a sooth ride. The only thing its missing is more power.
